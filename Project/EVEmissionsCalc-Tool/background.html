Background

Problem Statement

To educate consumers on the effective environmental and financial impact of their vehicle use.

Consumers do not have easy access to the data regarding the entire environmental and fiscal impact of their vehicle purchases. This tool will provide a single source, with easy to interoperate visualizations to provide consumers with all the information they need to decide on a purchase.

The Project

The project can be split up into two distinct parts. The front end, and the back end as seen below.

The front end is what you see here, the visual representation of data, words, and interactivity. Our inovative approach allows you to quickly and easily comapare different vehicle technologies and their corresponding emissions

The back end is where the magic happens. This is where we connect to the various databases, clean the data, and perform calculations to make the visualizations on your screen.
